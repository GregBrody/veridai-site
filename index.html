What it does

Three things, done well.

01 — Scan

Point your camera

Snap a photo of a paper contract or upload a PDF. Built-in OCR handles the rest, in five languages.

02 — Analyze

Read between the lines

An AI walks you through the document — flags risky clauses, hidden fees, exit conditions, and what they mean in plain language.

03 — Ask

Question anything

Wondering what a specific clause really means? Ask. VeridAI answers based on the actual contents of your contract.

Privacy first
